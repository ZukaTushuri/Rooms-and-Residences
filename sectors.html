Sector Detail

What we deliver in each sector.

01

Residential

Premium apartment fit-outs and residential renovations — complete interior design, sourcing and installation. Ideal for premium apartment owners and buyers seeking a finished, move-in-ready home to an international standard.

02

Villas

Large-format private villa projects — architecture, interiors, landscaping coordination, furniture, lighting, materials and full turnkey execution. Single-family residences where every detail is curated from brief to handover.

03

Hospitality

Hotels, boutique resorts and serviced apartment brands — from lobby and F&B to guest rooms and operational back-of-house. We understand hospitality-grade durability, brand standards and FF&E specification at scale.

04

Commercial

Retail, showrooms, restaurants and commercial interiors — designed to perform operationally and impress commercially. We deliver fast, coordinated fit-outs that meet brand guidelines and technical requirements.

05

Office Spaces

Corporate and co-working environments — space planning, furniture specification, lighting design and installation. Workplaces that support productivity, reflect brand culture and create the right first impression for clients and talent.

06

Medical & Wellness

Clinics, spas, wellness centres and medical facilities — interiors that combine clinical compliance with premium aesthetics. Materials, layouts and finishes specified for hygiene, durability and a calming patient experience.

07

Developer Projects

Multi-unit residential and mixed-use developments — show apartments, common areas and full-floor fit-outs at volume. Our procurement network and project management capability are built for the repetition and pace of developer timelines.

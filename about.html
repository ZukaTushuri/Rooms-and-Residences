Our Vision

Design-driven spaces, delivered without compromise.

We founded Rooms & Residences on a simple belief: clients deserve international-standard interiors without juggling a dozen suppliers, contractors and shipping agents. So we built one platform that owns the entire process.

From the first concept sketch to the final styling, a dedicated team carries your project — protecting the design intent, the budget and the timeline at every step.

The result is a space that looks the way it was imagined, delivered when it was promised, with a single point of accountability throughout.

What Defines Us

Built on four commitments.

International partnerships

Direct relationships with manufacturers and makers across Europe, Turkey and China — giving every project access to quality without the import mark-up of a distributor.

Full-service capability

Architecture, interiors, products, logistics and turnkey build under one roof — not outsourced to separate agencies but managed by one integrated team.

Quality standards

Specified, inspected and installed to a consistent premium benchmark — we don't ship product that hasn't been quality-checked, and we don't close a project without a full snagging sign-off.

Trust & professionalism

Transparent management, clear progress reporting and accountable delivery — clients always know where their project stands and what comes next.

Large-scale capability

Resourced and networked to deliver multi-unit developer projects and hospitality programmes at scale — not just individual residences.

Design + execution unity

What we draw, we can build. Design decisions are made with procurement and logistics reality in mind — so the concept and the completed space are always aligned.
